Projects I created

TaskRPG

TaskRPG is a unique productivity tool that combines task management with role-playing game elements. It aims to make task completion more engaging and fun by rewarding users with points and achievements as they complete their tasks.

Projects I contribute to

tldr-pages

The tldr-pages project provides simplified and community-driven man pages. It's a collection of concise and simplified help pages for command-line tools, providing practical examples for common use cases.

OpenStreetMap

OpenStreetMap is a collaborative project to create a free, editable map of the world. Contributors use GPS devices, aerial imagery, and other free sources to add and edit map data, creating a detailed and accurate world map.

OpenFoodFacts

OpenFoodFacts is a free, open database of food products from around the world. Contributors add product information, including ingredients, nutritional facts, and barcodes, helping people make informed food choices.
